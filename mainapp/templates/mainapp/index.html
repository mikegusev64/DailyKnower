What is DailyKnower?

We aim to give everyone a voice. Specifically those who have previously been afraid to use theirs. DailyKnower stands behind every persons right to an opinion and aims to fight the ongoing battle for free-speech. Whilst living in times on increased censorship, we aim to educate and provide unbiased resources for both; historical events, as well as events happening right in front of us. With platforms such as X revolutionizing media and the way people source their news, we at DailyKnower aim to play our part in ensuring you have a space to learn and express your opinions. As trust in legacy media is slowly being lost, your voice and opinions becomes more and more valuable... So use it.

DailyKnower is a fully anonymous platform, so all that is required of you is to think of a username and password for the sake of storing your notes within your "account".
